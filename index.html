What is Business Intelligence?

Business Intelligence connects:

Skills, technologies, applications and practices for the continuous iterative exploration and investigation of data, to get insight and drive business plan objectives.


Business Intelligence basically extract important insights from structured and unstructured data and transform them into valuable business information.

Companies can use this information to make strategic decisions, optimize operational efficiency and enhance productivity.

Data Visualization?

Data visualization helps users understand the significance of data, by placing it in a visual context.


To communicate information clearly and efficiently, data visualization uses statistical graphics, plots, information graphics and other tools.


Effective visualization helps users analyze and think about data and evidence. It makes complex data more accessible, understandable and usable.
